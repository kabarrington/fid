Kelly A. Barrington

Helping people make healthcare websites as inviting and user-friendly as possible, to maximize market share and increase profitability.

Kelly Barrington is a clinical psychologist and graphic designer. Her web design for healthcare companies focuses on Interaction Design and User Experience Design. After 14 years providing direct service to patients of all ages,
Kelly changed her focus from patient care to creating and managing websites for small to medium-size practices within the greater Orlando area. Kelly identifies the core values and goals of each healthcare practioner
or group of healthcare practitioners and designs a unique solution to advertising, web-scheduling, web-hosting, and providing assesment measures and surveys online.
